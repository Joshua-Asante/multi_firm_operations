## Methodological notes

1. **OANDA-proxy scope correction.** Brief preamble inaccurately labelled this
   as a "Pepperstone-canonical bar-corpus Notice pass". The Identify corpus
   is OANDA-proxy per the AMENDMENT. All findings carry `canonical_status =
   "PROXY"` and route as OANDA-only patterns. No finding here is eligible
   for Action routing or for lock-decision use without re-running on
   Pepperstone. Surfaced rather than silently substituted (AMENDMENT
   §"Forbidden-D-test discipline").
2. **Independence of this scan from 04-26 verdicts.** The same OANDA corpus
   was scanned 2026-04-26 with a different criterion set (D1–J3). Several
   anomalies surfaced here (Guardian Mon-H08 negative rejected R; Aegis
   in_session drift; Striker funnel concentration) coincide with 04-26
   observations. This is independent re-discovery on shared substrate, not
   citation. The brief explicitly forbade carrying forward 04-26 verdicts.
3. **Striker base-leg sim caveat.** O1 and O5 results for Striker use sim_R
   that excludes the 350% pyramid (declared Identify simplification —
   pyramid is "the entire structural edge" per Pine v4.4 header). Striker
   anomaly absence in O1 and Striker filter-contribution magnitudes in O5
   are upper-bounded interpretively as "no anomaly above threshold under
   base-leg sim", not under the full strategy.
4. **Aegis Q15 reference.** O3 Aegis flag flagged per brief instruction;
   `s2_in_session` 6mo period rate grew ~33% on the same panel where Q15
   observed Aegis PF 2.46 → 5.96 across early/mid/late panel sub-periods.
   The co-movement is descriptive — mechanism (whether the in-session-rate
   growth and Aegis PF improvement are the same signal) is synthesis-phase.
5. **O5 sign-reversal vs magnitude-drift.** O5's flag criterion (sign
   reversal) catches Guardian `block_MonH09` and Aegis `block_TueH10`. The
   Striker filters all show monotonic *magnitude* growth (e.g.,
   `block_warmup` −156R → −212R) without sign reversal. This pattern is
   visible in the per-third table but not flagged under the brief's
   sign-reversal criterion. If synthesis prefers a magnitude-drift criterion,
   the data is in the per-third table and JSON output.
6. **O6 partial-period caveat.** Period 8 (p8) is partial (~3.5 of 6
   months). The flagged Guardian +1.98 / Aegis −1.99 candidate is conditional
   on this partial-window value persisting; if the next ~2.5 months reverse
   the trend, p8 would re-evaluate. No mechanism proposed.
7. **Forbidden D-tests** — none triggered during this run. The OANDA-proxy
   scope-correction surfacing (top of file) is the boundary maintenance
   the AMENDMENT requires. No clusters deleted from O4 on macro-event
   grounds (FOMC/BOJ/NFP) — preserved with a tag column per brief instruction.
8. **Thin-cohort summary.**
   - O1: no thin sub-cohorts (smallest n=25).
   - O3: no thin periods (smallest universe count > 30).
   - O4: thin floor n=50 satisfied (n_adv=213).
   - O5: 5 of 18 (Guardian × third) cells thin (n<10), Rule 1 flagged.
   - O6: p8 partial-period caveat noted; no formal thin failure.
